About IVP

We are the Intelligent Video Perception Group, from the School of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Peking University, Shenzhen, China.

We are focusing on the research topics of Intelligent Video Coding, Image Processing & Computer Vision and Machine Learning & Deep Learning, including advanced algorithms and optimization models, and efficient implementations of practical Intelligent Systems.

Intelligent Video Coding

2D/3D/Intelligent/Perceptual Video Coding Optimization, Immersive Media and Virtual Reality (Panorama/Light Field/Point Cloud), Multimedia Streaming and Communication

Image Processing & Computer Vision

Image and Video Restoration, Salient Object Detection, Visual Perception Models and Visual Quality Enhancement

Machine Learning & Deep Learning & Intelligent Systems

Deep Neural Network Design and Architecture Optimization, Machine Learning and Optimization Methods, Multimedia and AI Accelerators, VLSI Architectures for ASIC/SoC/FPGA Design, HW/SW Co-design
